## Welcome

Welcome to the **YASARA Molecular Docking & Molecular Dynamics Tutorial**, a comprehensive, step-by-step guide designed to help beginners and researchers perform **Structure-Based Drug Design (SBDD)** using **YASARA Structure**.

This repository covers the complete computational workflow—from protein preparation and molecular docking to Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations and trajectory analysis—using practical examples and reproducible procedures. The tutorial is intended for self-learning, workshops, classroom instruction, and research applications in computational chemistry, structural biology, medicinal chemistry, and bioinformatics.

Whether you are new to molecular modeling or looking for a practical reference, this guide provides a structured workflow that can be adapted to a wide range of protein targets and ligands.

## What You Will Learn

By completing this tutorial, you will learn how to:

- Install and configure YASARA Structure
- Prepare protein structures from the Protein Data Bank (PDB)
- Validate molecular docking protocols
- Perform molecular docking simulations
- Analyze protein–ligand interactions
- Prepare ligands from PubChem
- Perform Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations
- Analyze MD trajectories using RMSD, RMSF, Radius of Gyration (Rg), SASA, and Hydrogen Bond analysis
- Apply best practices for reproducible computational research
